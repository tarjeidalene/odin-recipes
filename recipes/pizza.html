Pizza recipe

Description

This classic cheese pizza features a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted mozzarella cheese. Perfect for a quick and satisfying homemade meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • Flour (for dusting)

Steps to prepare

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. On a floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a thin, round shape.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a lightly oiled baking sheet or pizza peel if using a stone.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  6. Optionally, add a sprinkle of oregano and garlic powder for extra flavor.
  7. Bake the pizza for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
