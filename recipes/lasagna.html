Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese, typically baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce:
    • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage (or a mix)
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
    • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • For the Cheese Mixture:
    • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/2 tsp salt

How to

  1. Prepare the sauce: Cook ground beef or vegetables with tomato sauce, garlic, onions, and seasonings. Simmer for a rich flavor.
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture: Mix ricotta cheese (or béchamel), egg, and Parmesan for a creamy layer.
  3. Boil the pasta: Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside (or use no-boil noodles).
  4. Layer the ingredients: In a baking dish, layer sauce, pasta, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until full.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-40 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Rest & serve: Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
