Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese, typically baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage (or a mix)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to prepare

  1. Cook ground beef or vegetables with tomato sauce, garlic, onions, and seasonings. Simmer for a rich flavor.
  2. Mix ricotta cheese (or béchamel), egg, and Parmesan for a creamy layer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside (or use no-boil noodles).
  4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, pasta, cheese mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until full.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 30-40 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
