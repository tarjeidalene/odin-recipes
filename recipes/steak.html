Steak recipe

Description

A juicy and flavorful steak, seared to perfection, seasoned simply, and served with your favorite sides. This easy recipe will guide you to making a restaurant-quality steak at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks (about 1-inch thick)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika (optional)
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)

Steps to prepare

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature (about 30 minutes).
  2. Pat the steaks dry with a paper towel and season both sides with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Heat a heavy skillet (cast iron preferred) over high heat and add olive oil.
  4. Once the oil is hot, place the steaks in the pan and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  5. Reduce heat to medium, add butter, garlic, and rosemary/thyme, and baste the steak with the melted butter.
  6. Cook until the desired doneness is reached (use a meat thermometer: 130°F for medium-rare, 140°F for medium).
  7. Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite sides and enjoy!
